Supported Diseases

A common fungal disease causing dark spots on leaves and fruit.

A serious disease causing brown lesions and rapid leaf decay.

Leaf Mold

Yellow spots on leaves, often with a fuzzy mold underneath.

🍂
Septoria Leaf Spot

Small, circular spots with dark borders on lower leaves.

Water-soaked spots on leaves and fruit, turning brown and dry.

🦠
Bacterial Speck

Tiny black specks on leaves, stems, and fruit.

Upward curling and yellowing of leaves, stunted growth.

Mosaic Virus

Mottled, light and dark green patterns on leaves.
